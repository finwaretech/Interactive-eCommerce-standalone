At a Glance

The Happyland London Bus set is great for transporting your Happyland figures on a tour around the town to see the sights or to all their favourite events

Features and benefits for Happyland London Bus Set

  • Great for:
  • Driving around the room
  • Picking up and dropping off passengers
  • Encouraging talking and learning new words
  • Adding your own brrm and beep noises
  • Getting confident as you play
  • Discovering pretend play
  • Developing strength and precision in little fingers
  • Enjoying playing together.
  • Requires AA x2 batteries (included)
Want To Know More?

The Happyland London Bus set is great for transporting your Happyland figures to see all the famous sights and events. Lift the top deck to access the lower deck, put the passengers on the upper deck, listen to fun bus sounds and get on and off the bus.

Tickets please:
Be the tour guide on a fun trip around London. Put the passengers on the top deck for the best views, but they might get rained on.

Magical journeys:
Drive over bridges, round roundabouts, through tunnels and wave to the Queen at Buckingham Palace. Take the bus on exciting tours – at home, in London or into space.

Set includes the Happyland bus with fun bus sounds, a bus stop sign to let the driver know where to pick up their passengers and 5 Happyland characters.

Suitable from 18 months.

Don't Forget...

We have more from the Happyland range to add to your child's collection (available separately).

What's in the box:

  • 1 x Bus
  • 1 x Bus stop
  • 5 x Characters
